About

 Ringring.io

Bunch of features to give you the privacy in your secret calls and messages.

  Self destruct messages

Secret messages will self destruct automatically once when it's read.

 End to end secured calls

Secret calls are protected by the ZRTP protocol and it is guaranteed there is no man or company in the middle with spying activities.

 Self destruct call logs

Call logs on your device will self destruct automatically once when it's read.

 Do not store your activities

The Ringring.io servers are working only as a media proxy and VoIP registar. We simply do not have the capacity or technical requirements to store or analyse the highly protected activities.

 No man in the middle

Thanks to the highly secured ZRTP protocol your generated keys are sent directly to your partner and not through the Ringring.io servers.

 One address book

The contacts are all in the same address book what you got used to and no pressure to create a second one with a subset of your friends.
